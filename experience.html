Experiences

"Experiences" refer to the collective set of events, actions, and situations that an individual encounters and goes through in their life. These experiences can encompass a wide range of activities, emotions, and circumstances, both positive and negative. Experiences shape a person's perspectives, beliefs, values, and character, and they play a crucial role in personal growth and development.

MSAM volunteer

April 2019
"My volunteer experience at Minggu Saham Amanah Berhad 2019 was truly enriching. It provided me with a unique opportunity to actively participate in promoting financial literacy and investment education among the public. Assisting in organizing workshops, engaging with attendees, and witnessing their eagerness to learn about investment strategies was incredibly rewarding. This experience not only enhanced my communication and teamwork skills but also instilled in me a deep sense of social responsibility. It was a privilege to be part of an event dedicated to empowering individuals with financial knowledge, and I am grateful for the valuable lessons and memories from my time as a volunteer."

Alim Harmony Part Time Staff

November 2018 - March 2019
"My time as a part-timer at the bookstore was both enjoyable and educational. Working among shelves filled with books of diverse genres allowed me to cultivate a deep appreciation for literature and an understanding of customer preferences. Assisting customers in finding their favorite reads, recommending novels, and organizing book displays were all aspects I cherished. Moreover, it honed my organizational skills and patience in a dynamic retail environment. Being surrounded by book enthusiasts and connecting with fellow readers made this part-time role a memorable and intellectually stimulating experience."

Part Time Delivery Rider

July 2022 - September 2022
"My time as a part-time delivery service provider was a rewarding and fast-paced experience. Being responsible for ensuring timely and accurate deliveries to customers was both challenging and fulfilling. It taught me the importance of punctuality, attention to detail, and effective route planning. Interacting with customers and witnessing their satisfaction upon receiving their packages was a highlight of the role. This experience enhanced my communication skills and problem-solving abilities, and it gave me a deeper appreciation for the logistics and customer service aspects of the delivery industry."
